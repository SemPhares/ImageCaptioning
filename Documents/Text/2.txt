ABSTRACT
New deep-towed (PASISAR) seismic data of the KAIKO-TOKA1’96 cruise UUVL’Atalante), combined
with SAR side-scan sonar, multibeam bathymetric data and previously available data, confirm that the
backstop of the eastern Nankai wedge, close to the Izu collision zone, is affected by pervasive
right-lateral strike-slip faulting. We interpret this tectonic pattern as the result of the combination of
shear partitioning due to oblique subduction with the effect of the Izu collision. 0 Acadi%ie des
